Passport Photo Generator

Python, OpenCV, Node.js, Javascript, Express, HTML

Passport Photo Generator is a locally hosted website which converts inputted images into passport photos satisfying U.S. passport requirements. It utilizes OpenCV for face detection, image rotation, centering and alignment, and the U2-net deep learning architecture for salient object detection for background removal. The above image was generated using the program!

Waitlist Bot

Node.js, discord.js, JavaScript

Waitlist-Bot is a discord bot that assists with the course waitlist process at UIUC. It takes into account that most students intend to drop a course they're currently in if a seat opens up in their desired course, and then utilizes this knowledge to construct a directed multigraph where each edge connects a student's target course to the course they're willing to drop. It then uses a DFS to detect cycles and notify all involved users once found, enabling them to all register for their desired courses.

ISEF SOFT036 - Improved Quantum Cryptography

Python, Qiskit, MatPlotLib, IBM Q

SOFT036 was a research project done by myself and Rohan Kulkarni(Columbia '25) for the 2021 Regeneron International Science and Engineering Fair. Our project centered on designing and implementing an improvement to the BB84 quantum key distribution protocol, specifically targeting its inefficient retention rate through the use of quantum entangled systems and improving its security by devising a quantum digital signature scheme to verify the sender's identity without leaking information to an eavesdropper.

2021 MIT Battlecode

Java

Battlecode is a real-time strategy game where teams of up to four code an AI player. The AI player needs to manage a robot army and control their behaviors individually and as a collective with limited communication and computational abilities in order to defeat the enemy player. Our bot utilized pathfinding, distributed algorithms, communications, and other strategies to remain competitive. We placed 22nd out of over 550 total teams(high school and college), and 4th out of 52 teams in the H.S. division.
