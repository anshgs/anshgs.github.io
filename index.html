Recent Projects

Style Share

JavaScript, Node, React, Redux, tfjs


Style Share is a website that allows users to create 3d scenes and use a machine learning model to stylize them according to the style of another image. The model was built using tfjs to allow it to run within the browser, and was compressed by nearly 8x using quantization and distillation to increase its speed. You can test out the website here!

Infected & Detected

TFLite, OpenCV, Flask, MongoDB
HackIllinois 2022: Best Community & Sustainability Track Project

An edge computing tool to help farmers get analytics on their crop health and invasive weed growth over time. An image classifier was trained using transfer learning on MobileNetV2 to identify different types of plant diseases and weeds. We utilized pruning and quantization afterwards to compress the model by 10x to run quickly on a Rasberry Pi with a Coral Edge TPU.

EscapeMaze

C++, OpenGL, CMake, Catch2

EscapeMaze is a Maze game where you're tasked with escaping a randomly regenerating maze while enemy AI chase you down. The game was built as a final project for CS128 @UIUC entirely using C++ and the OpenGL library. It generates mazes using a DFS-based maze generation algorithm, while allowing AI's to search through the maze with a BFS.

Passport Photo Generator

Python, OpenCV, Node.js, Javascript, Express, HTML

Passport Photo Generator is a locally hosted website which converts inputted images into passport photos satisfying U.S. passport requirements. It utilizes OpenCV for face detection, image rotation, centering and alignment, and the U2-net deep learning architecture for salient object detection for background removal. The above image was generated using the program!

Waitlist Bot

Node.js, discord.js, JavaScript

Waitlist-Bot is a discord bot that assists with the course waitlist process at UIUC. It takes into account that most students intend to drop a course they're currently in if a seat opens up in their desired course, and then utilizes this knowledge to construct a directed multigraph where each edge connects a student's target course to the course they're willing to drop. It then uses a DFS to detect cycles and notify all involved users once found, enabling them to all register for their desired courses.

ISEF SOFT036 - Improved Quantum Cryptography

Python, Qiskit, MatPlotLib, IBM Q

SOFT036 was a research project for the 2021 Regeneron International Science and Engineering Fair. Our project centered on designing and implementing an improvement to the BB84 quantum key distribution protocol, specifically targeting its inefficient retention rate through the use of quantum entangled systems and improving its security by devising a quantum digital signature scheme to verify the sender's identity without leaking information to an eavesdropper.

Mathworks Math Modeling Challenge

Python, C++

The M3 Challenge is a math modeling competition where teams of 6 are tasked to collect data, design models, and write a paper to solve various tasks. The 2021 competition centered around combatting the "digital divide", with questions on predicting the cost of the internet over the next decade, predicting the needed bandwidth to support a household given information on its inhabitants, and optimizing the distribution of 5G across a hypothetical region. Our team's paper placed within the top 100 out of nearly 600 submission.

2021 MIT Battlecode

Java

Battlecode is a real-time strategy game where teams of up to four code an AI player. The AI player needs to manage a robot army and control their behaviors individually and as a collective with limited communication and computational abilities in order to defeat the enemy player. Our bot utilized pathfinding, distributed algorithms, communications, and other strategies to remain competitive. We placed 22nd out of over 550 total teams(high school and college), and 4th out of 52 teams in the H.S. division.
